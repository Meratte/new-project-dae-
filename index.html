GoPassion!

GoPassion provides a lot of resources for people who might not know what they want to do in the future. We will present you with articles and fun facts about different paths you can take, and you get to choose whether you want to continue learning it or do something else!

Why should you use us?

  • We are a non-profit organization that tries to help students choose the right track for them.
  • Do you know that according to LinkedIn, 75 percent of 25-33 year olds don't know what their dream job is?

So, if you're a teen or someone who's in their early-mid 20s, you should consider using our product! Now go and explore different passions!

You got here because you have these many passions, but you don't know which one is going to be sustainable over time, what the job market is like for this, and most importantly - how do you choose one?
Our product offers it all - type one job and get an output on research articles, salaries, and requirements for the job! And, if you're hungry for even more, you can also explore other careers that will be mapped with one of your passions.
Let's say that you're interested in the arts - you can choose it as a passion and get a list of careers as well as resources on how you can get there. If for whatever reason you decide not to explore it further (our quizzes won't do good or you get bored) you can always change it and focus on one passion at a time!

Demo of our product!

Modeled with the help of Large Language Models, we provide you with our own machine girl - Gesa! She'll be your assistant and will help you navigate through the passions so you can choose one you like and explore it!
